About GraabGen

Revolutionizing Attendance Management with AI

GraabGen was founded in 2023 with a vision to transform traditional attendance systems into intelligent, automated solutions. Our team of experts in artificial intelligence, software development, and education technology came together to create a product that saves time, increases accuracy, and provides valuable insights.

Using state-of-the-art face recognition algorithms, our system ensures that attendance is taken in seconds, eliminating manual errors and proxy attendance. We are committed to enhancing the educational experience by allowing teachers to focus on teaching rather than administrative tasks.

Our mission is to become the leading provider of smart attendance solutions, empowering educational institutions worldwide with technology that is both innovative and reliable.
